Your geography is important! Walk, Ride, Bike,
Car Share, or Take Public Transit.

We build our communities in vibrant cities close to employers, activities, cultural happenings, transit options, and great local stores. Nestled in luscious green space, The Royal Belmont offers a serene community not too far from Boston. Located a short distance from Alewife Train Station, you are just a train ride away from great shopping and dining in Boston. The Minuteman Commuter Bikeway entrance is also minutes away providing access to 10 miles of biking path through Cambridge, Arlington, Lexington and Bedford. So leave your car at home and get out and explore your city.
